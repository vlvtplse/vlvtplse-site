01 — The Artists

VLVT//PLSE came to life in the charged hours following the temple burn at LoveBurn, when Kirbie took the mic for the first time. What began as a single moment became a project, and a project became a mission — to build musical journeys that transform dancefloors into shared rituals of movement, emotion, and connection. Justin's architectural instinct behind the decks and Kirbie's rare contralto voice don't just complement each other. They complete the pulse.

Kirbie

aka Velvet

Kirbie's contralto voice brings a rare warmth to the world of electronic music. Raised in Canada singing country music around campfires, she later sang in a rock band on a tiny Caribbean island before finding her way into the underground scene in Tulum. Sultry, rich, and deeply felt, her vocals don't sit on top of a set — they move through it, bringing a fun, feminine energy and pulling listeners into presence and emotion. Grounded enough to feel, light enough to carry you somewhere else.

Justin

aka Quantify

Raised in Belgium during the emerging 90s rave scene and trained in piano from a young age, Justin arrived at electronic music from both ends — feeling it in rooms before he understood it, then learning to build it from the inside out. His sets move with architectural precision, weaving through genres and energy levels to create tension, release, and euphoria. An emerging Beatport artist with releases on Kitu Records, he has performed at Burning Man and iconic venues across Tulum, Chicago, and beyond.
